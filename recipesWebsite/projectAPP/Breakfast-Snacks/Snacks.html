Bruschetta

A classic Italian appetizer featuring diced tomatoes, fresh basil, and garlic on toasted bread.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Baguette slices, toasted

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.

2. Allow the mixture to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

3. Toast baguette slices until golden brown.

4. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.

5. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing flavors of Bruschetta!

Spinach and Feta Phyllo Triangles

Flaky phyllo pastry filled with a savory mixture of spinach and feta cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package phyllo pastry sheets
  • 1 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • Butter for brushing

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).

2. In a bowl, combine thawed and drained spinach, feta cheese, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and chopped dill.

3. Lay out a sheet of phyllo pastry and brush it with melted butter. Repeat with additional layers.

4. Place a spoonful of the spinach and cheese mixture at one end of the phyllo strip.

5. Fold the phyllo over the filling to form a triangle, continuing to fold until the end of the strip.

6. Brush the triangles with butter and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

7. Serve warm and enjoy these delightful Spinach and Feta Phyllo Triangles!

Baked Sweet Potato Fries

Healthy and crispy sweet potato fries seasoned with a blend of spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, cut into fries
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 425째F (220째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. In a bowl, toss sweet potato fries with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.

3. Spread the seasoned fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.

4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the fries are golden and crispy.

5. Remove from the oven and serve immediately.

6. Enjoy these tasty and nutritious Baked Sweet Potato Fries!
