Classic Caprese Salad

Ingredients:
4 large tomatoes, sliced
200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
Extra virgin olive oil
Balsamic glaze
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving plate. Tuck fresh basil leaves between slices. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.


Avocado Toast
Ingredients
2 slices whole-grain bread, toasted
1 ripe avocado
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional toppings: poached egg, cherry tomatoes, chili flakes



Instructions:
Mash the ripe avocado and spread it evenly on toasted bread. Season with salt and pepper. Add your favorite toppings. Enjoy!


Grilled Chicken Salad
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Mixed salad greens
Cherry tomatoes, halved
Cucumber, sliced
Red onion, thinly sliced
Balsamic vinaigrette dressing
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper. Grill until cooked through. Slice chicken. Arrange salad greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion on a plate. Top with grilled chicken slices. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.


Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 carrot, grated
1 celery stalk, finely chopped
1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup red wine (optional)
2 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
Spaghetti noodles, cooked
Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
 Instructions:
In a large skillet, brown the ground beef. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until vegetables are softened. Stir in crushed tomatoes, red wine, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes. Serve over cooked spaghetti. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.


Grilled Salmon with Lemon Herb Sauce
Ingredients:
4 salmon fillets
2 tbsp olive oil
1 lemon, juiced and zested
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dried thyme
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh parsley for garnish

 Instructions:
Preheat the grill. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper. Brush the salmon fillets with the mixture. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through. Garnish with fresh parsley.


Vegetarian Quesadillas:
Ingredients:
Flour tortillas
1 cup black beans, cooked
1 cup corn kernels
1 bell pepper, diced
1 cup shredded cheese
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
Guacamole and salsa for serving

 Instructions:
Place a tortilla on a skillet over medium heat.Add black beans, corn, bell pepper, and cheese on one side. Sprinkle with cumin and chili powder,Fold the tortilla in half and cook until cheese is melted,Serve with guacamole and salsa.


Stir-Fried Shrimp and Vegetables:
Ingredients:
1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snow peas)
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oyster sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
Cooked rice for serving

 Instructions:
Heat sesame oil in a wok or skillet over high heat.Add shrimp and stir-fry until pink.Add mixed vegetables and garlic, stir-fry until vegetables are crisp-tender,Mix in soy sauce and oyster sauce,Serve over cooked rice
