Matcha marble cake:

Ingredients:
115g unsalted butter
200g granulated sugar
3 eggs
250g all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
180g greek yougert
10g matcha powder
60ml whole milk

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 180°C Whisk together the sifted matcha powder with the warm milk until the matcha is dissolved (set aside) In a bowl, mix the butter with the sugar Add the egg Then add the Greek yogurt Stir in the dry ingredients (flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda) Divide batter in half and add the matcha mixture to one of the bowls Alternate adding matcha batter and vanilla batter to the mold Bake in a 22 x 12 cm loaf pan at 180°C for 55 to 60 minutes Let the cake cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
cinnamon rolls:
Ingredients
Yeast mixture: 3/4 cup Lukewarm water
2 1/2 tsp Active dry yeast
1 tbsp Sugar
Rest of your dough ingredients:
1/3 cup Sugar
1 Egg whisked
1 Egg yolk whisked
3 cups All-purpose flour
1 tsp Kosher salt
4 tbsp Unsalted butter melted
Filling:
1/3 cup Unsalted butter room temperature
1 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp Cinnamon
Frosting:
4 oz Cream cheese room temp
3 tbsp Unsalted butter room temp
1 tsp Vanilla extract
Instructions:
combine the lukewarm water, active dry yeast, and 1 tbsp of sugar. Let it rest for 10 minutes, In a separate bowl, combine the remaining 1/3 cup of sugar, whisked eggs, all-purpose flour, kosher salt, and melted unsalted butter. Add in the yeast mixture last, Mix the dough ingredients, knead for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth, and let it rest in a warm place for to 2 hours, To make the filling, combine the room temperature unsalted butter, brown sugar, cinnamon To make the frosting, combine the room-temperature cream cheese, unsalted butter, vanilla extract, and sifted powdered sugar (added gradually) until you achieve the desired consistency. Once the dough has doubled in size, Roll it out and spread the filling over the dough, Cut the cinnamon rolls with floss into either 2-inch sections for a batch of 6, or 1-inch to 1 1/2-inch sections to make about 8-12 cinnamon rolls. Please place them in a prepared baking dish and cover them with a damp cloth. Let them rest for another 30 minutes, Preheat the oven to 350°F. Add a tablespoon of heavy cream on top of each cinnamon roll. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Once the cinnamon rolls are done, let them cool for a few minutes before topping them off with the frosting, and you're done!.
Brownies:
Ingredients
150g chocolate
160g white flour
250g cups caster sugar
120g unsalted butter
120ml nut milk
½ teaspoon mercury salt
1 teaspoon vanilla paste
60g cocoa powderbrbr
Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 180°C/356°F, line a 15cm square baking tray with baking paper, and grease the sides with baking paper. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Collecting. Then add the chocolate. Continue stirring until the chocolate melts. In a large bowl, sift the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Collecting. Pour the wet ingredients into the large bowl with the dry ingredients. mix up. Pour the mixture into the tray. Bake for 20 minutes. Remove the brownies and let them cool completely in the tin. Slice and enjoy!


Blueberry Muffins:
Ingredients
1 cup milk
1 large egg
⅓ cup vegetable oil
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
½ cup white sugar
½ cup fresh blueberries
 Instructions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.Stir together milk, egg, and oil in a large bowl. Add flour, baking powder, sugar, and blueberries; gently mix the batter with only a few strokes. Spoon batter into the muffin tin.Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Serve hot.

Strawberry Pie:
Ingredients:
1 pre-baked pie crust
4 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
1 cup granulated sugar
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 cup water
Whipped cream for serving (optional)
 Instructions:
In a saucepan, mash 1 cup of strawberries with a fork or potato masher. In a small bowl, mix sugar and cornstarch. Add the sugar mixture and water to the mashed strawberries. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes. Place the remaining fresh strawberries in the pre-baked pie crust. Pour the strawberry mixture over the fresh strawberries in the crust. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or until set. Serve slices with whipped cream if desired.


Tiramisu:
Ingredients:
1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
3 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
3 large eggs
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup mascarpone cheese
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
24-30 ladyfinger cookies
Cocoa powder for dusting
 Instructions:
parate bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until thick and pale. Add the mascarpone cheese and vanilla extract to the egg mixture. Mix until smooth. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are moist but not soaked. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish and spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to set. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder. Slice and enjoy your delicious Tiramisu!


Sticky Toffee Pudding:
Ingredients:
1 cup dates, chopped
1 cup boiling water
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
Pinch of salt
Toffee Sauce:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
 Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish.Mix dates with boiling water and baking soda. Let sit for 5 minutes: cream butter and brown sugar. Add eggs, one at a time. Combine dry ingredients, then fold in the date mixture. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Toffee Sauce: Melt butter, add cream and brown sugar. Simmer for 5-7 minutes. Stir in vanilla extract. Serving: Poke holes in warm pudding, and pour over some toffee sauce. Serve with extra sauce on the side. Enjoy your quick Sticky Toffee Pudding!
